Holi

Holi (Hindi pronunciation: ['hoːli:]) is a popular and significant Hindu festival celebrated as the Festival of Colours, Love, and Spring

Onam

Onam (IPA: [oːɳɐm]) is an annual Indian harvest and cultural festival related to Hinduism celebrated mostly by the people of Kerala.


Diwali

Diwali (English: /dɪˈwɑːliː/; Deepavali[3] (IAST: dīpāvalī) or Divali;[a]) is the Hindu festival of lights with its variations also celebrated in other Indian religions.
